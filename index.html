In the quantum information theory group of Prof. Ignacio Cirac, I work on quantum foundations, in particular conditions for quantum violations of classical behavior in macroscopic systems. Before, I worked on field of quantum magnetomechanics, where we proposed experimental setups using the magnetic properties of superconductors to observe quantum behavior on massive objects.
